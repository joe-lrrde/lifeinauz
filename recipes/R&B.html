Risotto & Mushrooms

Ingredients

Steps

Heat stock at high heat, until almost boiling. Remove from heat
Add oil, onion, rice and wine. Cook until wine is mostly evaporated.
Add stock, laddle by laddle. waiting for the rice to have absorbed the previous laddle before adding one. While this is happening, cook garlic with oil and keep.
cook mushrooms on high heat, after a bit, add garlic
Mix everything together and you're all done !
PSA : risotto rice takes quite a long time to cook so don't be too ambitious on the quantity except if you want to spend a fair amount of time in the kitchen ':)
