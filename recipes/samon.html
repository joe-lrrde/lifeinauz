Salmon and Spinach pasta

Ingredients

Steps

Chop spinach leaves
Add chopped onion, garlic, pepper and solt to medium heat oiled pan for 4-5 minutes.Until soft.
add spinach and leave on for 3 minutes.
add cream cheese and then remove from heat. Add parmesan and if possible, blend in a blender to make a creamy sauce.
Cook pasta and in a pot add the sauce and salmon with 1/3 of pasta water. Add salt and pepper according to your taste
You're all done !
